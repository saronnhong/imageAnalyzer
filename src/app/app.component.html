Image Analyzer


Choose an image for upload to ImageAnalyzer.

ImageAnalyzer will then transfer the image to an S3 bucket and leverage AWS Rekognition's Detect Faces functionality. This process delivers a comprehensive image analysis, including details on gender, age, smiling expressions, emotions, eyeglasses or sunglasses presence, beard or mustache recognition, open eyes, and mouth status.

The outcomes of this analysis are stored neatly in a MariaDB SQL database.
